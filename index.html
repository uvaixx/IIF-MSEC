Who We Are

About MSEC-IIF

The MSEC Innovation & Incubation Foundation (MSEC IIF) is a dynamic innovation ecosystem established at Mohamed Sathak Engineering College, Kilakarai, with a mission to nurture entrepreneurial talent, support startup creation, and drive technology-led innovation.

MSEC IIF serves as a platform for students, faculty, and aspiring entrepreneurs to transform ideas into viable products and scalable businesses. The centre focuses on fostering a culture of innovation through structured mentorship, technical support, industry collaboration, and access to modern prototyping facilities.

Vision

  • To become a leading incubation centre that empowers innovators to build sustainable and impactful startups contributing to national and global development.

Mission

  • To promote innovation and entrepreneurial thinking among students and faculty.
  • To support early-stage startups through mentorship, funding access, and infrastructure
  • To bridge the gap between academia and industry
  • To enable technology commercialization and job creation

Core Objectives

  • To nurture early-stage innovative ideas and convert them into viable prototypes.
  • To build entrepreneurial skills through structured mentoring, training, and capacity-building programs.
  • To facilitate the progression of innovators toward startup creation and incubation.

Pathways

Our Programs

A structured continuum from idea discovery to market-ready venture — designed for students, faculty innovators, and early-stage entrepreneurs.

Stage 01

Idea Lab & Pre-Incubation

The entry point for first-time innovators. Discover your problem-solving potential, validate your idea, and build your first prototype under guided mentorship.

  • Design Thinking & Ideation Bootcamps
  • Problem Statement Identification
  • Lean Canvas & Business Model Workshop
  • Peer Mentoring & Faculty Connect
  • Structured Innovation Review & Feedback Sessions
  • Access to Prototype Lab
Stage 02

Incubation Program

For teams with a validated prototype ready to build a Minimum Viable Product and test market fit. Get workspace, funding, and curated mentor access.

  • Dedicated Co-working Space
  • Industry Mentor Pairing
  • MVP Development Support
  • IP Filing & Patent Assistance
  • Funding Connect (DST, MSME, BIRAC)
  • Product Testing & Market Research
Stage 03

Acceleration & Scale

For early-revenue startups ready to scale operations, attract investors, and expand into new markets with institutional and network support.

  • Investor Pitch & Demo Days
  • National Incubator Networking
  • Legal, Finance & Compliance
  • DPIIT Startup India Recognition
  • Alumni Entrepreneur Network
  • Export & Commercialisation Support

Ministry of Education

Institutional Innovation Council (IIC)

MSEC's Institutional Innovation Council (IIC) is established under the Ministry of Education's Innovation Cell (MoE's IC) initiative to systematically foster the culture of Innovation and Entrepreneurship among students.

The IIC conducts a minimum of 25 prescribed activities per year including hackathons, idea competitions, innovation lectures, and policy sensitization workshops, earning performance-based ratings from MoE annually.
